Hello!

As a teenager I wanted to be many things. I knew I loved maths, technical drawing and mechanical engineering. I also liked architecture, so I studied Civil and Architectural Engineering. I specialised in structural engineering for complex geometry buildings. Over the years, working as an engineer and later on managing projects and teams in engineering consultancies, I started questioning why the companies I worked for made the decisions they made growing an interest in business. To pursue this interest I studied an MBA which allowed me to have a 360 view on businesses.

Soon after the MBA the importance of DATA sank deep. I knew that for a business to be succesful today it needs to be run by data-driven people. Not only the best strategic decisions are founded on data but also the implementation of AI solutions for automizing processes will result in a more efficient structure. As I started digging into the world of data I got excited at the capabilities and the prospects of using data and ML in any industry, or just even in our daily lives! Hence I decided to train as a Data Scientist.

I learn about Data daily, the inmensity of the world of data amazes me! The technological advancements create a need to continous development that I really enjoy. The skills I developed during my past work experience support my new carreer.

As a Data Consultant I have learned to master data analytics, visualisations, supervised and unsupervised algorithms for ML models. I am now learning about cloud computing, big data and data engineering. You can see my projects on GitHub . You will find analytics and data science projects.
