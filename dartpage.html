Data as an art

When I started in the world of data I found visualisations super beautiful. I could see scatter plots as pieces of art, I could imagine them decorating the walls at home. In my imagination they made a beatiful piece of art that matched deco styles like nordic or modern. And what a nicer piece of art that one that speaks of yourself!!! So I started gathering loved ones data, making plots, found the right thickness paper for prints (after several tries) and framed them beautifully. What a better personalised gift? I also see it as my way of contributing to people being data-aware!
